WELCOME TO MASURIAN LAKE DISTRICT!

  Stare Sady is a lovely and quiet summer country resort village situated by the Tałty Lake in the middle of the Big Lakes Trail, 6 km from Mikołajki.

Our guesthouses: Mila, Mela and Morela are located 50m from the lake with the access to the beach (100m) with bathing waters suited also for children and equipped with a slip for launching and lifting boats. Near the beach there is a sailing yacht and motorboat marina - “Port Sady”, with a highly recommended restaurant “Pod Jabłoniami” and a cocktail bar “Mza” almost adjacent to it. Within the range of 5 km one can find numerous guesthouses and hotels with various restaurants, as well as an Aqua Park.

What is unique for us is our own helicopter landing place, so please arrive that way as well !
